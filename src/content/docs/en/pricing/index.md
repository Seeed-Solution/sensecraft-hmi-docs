## Beta Version

Currently, SenseCraft HMI is in **Beta** and is **free** to use.

### AI Generation Limits

- **Daily Limit**: Each account is limited to 10 AI-generated creations per day, including 5 image generation/adjustments and 5 canvas generation/adjustments
- **Reset Time**: Limits reset every 24 hours

### Image Upload Guidelines

- **Without an SD Card**: Up to 10 images can be uploaded.

- **With an SD Card Inserted**: Up to 20 images and an additional 3 files of other supported types can be uploaded.
